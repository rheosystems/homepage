Lean Manufacturing Projects

Lean Manufacturing is a set of methodologies, initially developed at Toyota, with the overall objective of eliminating wasteful activities in manufacturing processes.

We have years of experience with practical implementations on the shop floor and the results are generally measurable within 2-3 weeks.

Sustainable Business Improvement

The 20 Keys is an implementation approach used for implementing a comprehensive range of world class manufacturing practices.

Developed in Japan, the 20 keys is a step-by-step road map to world class manufacturing that include consulting, training and benchmarking.

This is offered in cooperation with our partner ODI.

Improvements in Development, Service and Acquisition Processes

Capability Maturity Model Integration (CMMI) is a process improvement framework for product development (CMMI-DEV), services (CMMI-SRV) and acquisitions (CMMI-ACQ).

CMMI is developed by a group of experts from industry, government, and the CMMI Institute at Carnegie Mellon University.

Training and consulting on CMMI is done in cooperation with JCSE.

Joburg Centre for Software Engineering
